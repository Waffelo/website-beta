New website! Again!!!
17th September 2022

Yes I know that I reworked my website a couple of months ago, I kept working on my website for couple of weeks and during that time I learned a lot of things. I really like how my v2 turned out, but after I gained more skills I felt like my website looks ugly, it looked really basic and boring, just one simple grey color and few emojis.
Because of that I have decided to start working on my new website, the third version! I took new approach to it and used a different style, I got inspired by github and put the website's content into outlined boxes. I also took something from the 00s websites, the sidebars and centered content. You can also see that I started to use blur and gradients more. The background has now checkerboard pattern that is animated.
